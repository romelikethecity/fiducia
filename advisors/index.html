Frequently Asked Questions

What types of financial advisors do you cover?

We cover every registered financial professional in America: RIAs, broker-dealer representatives, CFPs, wealth managers, insurance-licensed advisors, retirement plan specialists, estate planners, and hybrid/dual-registered advisors.

How is your data different from ZoomInfo or Apollo?

We build lists from FINRA BrokerCheck, SEC IAPD, and state regulatory databases. Every advisor is verified against their CRD record. Generic B2B tools tag everyone as 'financial services' with no registration verification.

Can I combine multiple advisor types in one list?

Yes. Tell us your criteria and we build a single list that spans multiple categories. For example, all CFP-credentialed advisors at independent RIAs with $50M-$500M AUM in the western United States.
