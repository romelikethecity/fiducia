Insurance-Licensed Advisor Data

Financial advisors holding state insurance licenses for life, annuity, variable, and long-term care products. Often dual-registered with FINRA.

Data Fields Available

Every insurance-licensed advisors record includes verified contact information and regulatory data sourced from FINRA, SEC, and state regulatory databases.

  • Identity: Full name, CRD number, firm name, firm CRD
  • Registration: License type, registration status, regulatory body (SEC vs state)
  • Credentials: CFP, CFA, ChFC, CIMA, AEP, and other professional designations
  • Financials: AUM range (from SEC ADV filings), fee structure
  • Contact: Verified email, direct phone, mailing address
  • Compliance: Disclosure history, complaint records, regulatory actions

How We Source Insurance-Licensed Advisors Data

Financial advisors holding state insurance licenses for life, annuity, variable, and long-term care products. Often dual-registered with FINRA.

We pull registration records from FINRA BrokerCheck and SEC IAPD, then enrich each record with verified contact information from multiple commercial and public sources. Every email is deliverability-tested. Every phone number is checked for connectivity. Inactive registrations are flagged or excluded.

Common Use Cases

Teams selling compliance technology, fintech platforms, insurance products, and advisory services use Fiducia insurance-licensed advisors data for targeted outreach. Custodians use it for RIA recruitment. Recruiting firms use it to source candidates. Asset managers use it to identify distribution partners.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many insurance-licensed advisors are in your database?

We cover every registered insurance-licensed advisor in America based on current FINRA and SEC filings. The exact count depends on your filters (geography, AUM, firm type). Contact us with your criteria for a count estimate.

Can I filter insurance-licensed advisors by geography?

Yes. Filter by state, metro area, zip code, or custom geography. We can also segment by firm office location vs advisor home office registration.

How current is the insurance-licensed advisors data?

Lists are built to order from current regulatory filings. FINRA and SEC databases update daily. Your list reflects active registration status as of the build date.
