Independent RIA Contact Data

Fiducia provides CRD-verified contact data for independent ria professionals across the United States. Every record is sourced from FINRA BrokerCheck, SEC IAPD, and state regulatory databases, then enriched with verified emails and direct phone numbers.

Owner-operated advisory firms managing assets independently without wirehouse affiliation. These advisors represent a specific segment within the broader registered investment advisors category, and our data reflects the nuances that matter for targeted outreach.

Available Data Fields

  • Identity: Full name, CRD number, firm name, firm CRD number
  • Registration: License type, active/inactive status, regulatory jurisdiction
  • Credentials: Professional designations (CFP, CFA, ChFC, and others)
  • Financial: AUM range from SEC ADV filings where available
  • Contact: Verified email address, direct phone number, mailing address
  • Compliance: Disclosure history and regulatory actions from BrokerCheck

Who Uses This Data

Sales teams at fintech companies, compliance technology vendors, custodians, asset managers, and insurance distributors use independent ria data to build targeted prospect lists. Recruiting firms use it to source candidates in this specialty.

The key advantage over generic B2B databases: we can isolate independent ria professionals specifically, verified against their actual regulatory filings. No guessing from job titles or LinkedIn profiles.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you identify independent ria advisors specifically?

We use a combination of CRD registration data, firm type classification, and regulatory filings to identify advisors operating as independent ria professionals. This goes beyond job titles to actual registration and practice characteristics.

Can I combine independent ria data with other filters?

Yes. Layer on geography, AUM range, credentials, firm size, or any other available field. We build custom lists to your exact specifications.

What format do I get the data in?

Standard delivery is a clean CSV file formatted for CRM import. We can also deliver in custom formats or push directly to your CRM via API on request.
