Apollo Alternative for Advisor Contacts

Apollo alternative for RIA and advisor contacts. CRD-verified data with AUM segmentation and no annual contracts.

Why Teams Look for a Apollo Alternative

If you sell to financial advisors, Apollo gives you a massive database but limited ability to filter for the contacts that actually matter. No CRD verification, no AUM segmentation, no license-type filtering. You're paying enterprise pricing for data that requires hours of manual cleanup.

Fiducia is built specifically for teams targeting financial advisors. Every record is verified against FINRA BrokerCheck and SEC IAPD. You can filter by registration type, AUM range, license series, credential, and firm structure. And you pay per contact, not per seat.

What Fiducia Offers Instead

  • CRD-verified contacts: Every advisor checked against regulatory databases
  • AUM segmentation: Filter by assets under management from SEC filings
  • License filtering: Series 7, 65, 66, state insurance licenses
  • No annual contracts: Pay per list, cancel anytime
  • Verified deliverability: 95%+ email deliverability rate
  • 3-5 day delivery: Custom lists built to your exact criteria
