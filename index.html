Frequently Asked Questions

What data fields are included in each advisor record?

Every record includes the advisor's full name, CRD number, registration type (RIA, BD, dual), license status, firm name, firm CRD, AUM range, verified email, direct phone, mailing address, credentials (CFP, CFA, etc.), and disclosure history. Custom fields available on request.

How do you verify advisor registrations?

We cross-reference every advisor against FINRA BrokerCheck and SEC IAPD in real time. Registration status, license type, firm affiliation, and disclosure history are all confirmed before delivery. Inactive or revoked licenses are flagged or excluded depending on your preference.

Can I filter advisors by AUM range?

Yes. We segment advisors by assets under management using regulatory filings. You can target specific bands, for example, RIAs managing between $50M and $500M, or solo advisors under $25M. AUM data comes from ADV filings with the SEC.

What license types can you segment by?

Series 7, Series 65, Series 66, Series 6, state insurance licenses (life, health, variable), and specialty registrations. You can also filter by credential: CFP, CFA, ChFC, CIMA, AEP, and others.

Do you include disclosure or complaint history?

Yes. Every advisor record can include disclosure data from FINRA BrokerCheck: customer complaints, regulatory actions, terminations, bankruptcies, and civil judgments. You can filter for clean records only or include all disclosures for compliance screening.

How fresh is the data?

Lists are built to order using current regulatory filings. FINRA and SEC databases update daily. Your list reflects registration status as of the build date. We don't resell stale bulk downloads.

What does 'no annual contracts' mean?

You pay per list, per project. No seat licenses, no platform fees, no 12-month commitments. Order once or order monthly. Your choice.
